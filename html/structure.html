<Header> Represents the header of a session.

<nav> represents the navigation bar.

<article> is used in HTML to mark up independent and self-contained content, such as a news story, a blog post, an opinion piece, and so on.

<section> works in a similar way to the <article> tag, but it is not self-sufficient and relates to the general content of the page, being a section of the whole.

<aside> is a content separate from the main content, whether related as an auxiliary to the page or content, such as quotes, notes, sidebars or even advertisements.

<dialog> represents an on-screen dialog box. An 'open' property defines whether the tag is visible or not.

<div> is a division tag, it's used to make generic screen and content divisions to organize it.

<footer> Represents the footer of a session.
